Comprehensive Cat Breeds Summary

Here's a quick overview of the most loved cat breeds worldwide, perfect for new owners.

  • Siamese: Elegant body, blue eyes, extremely vocal and attached to owners.
  • Persian: Long luxurious fur, gentle temperament, needs regular grooming.
  • Maine Coon: America's native giant, sociable, polydactyl paws common.
  • Bengal: Leopard-like spots, high energy, loves water.
  • Ragdoll: Goes limp when picked up, dog-like loyalty.
  • Sphynx: Wrinkle-skinned, very affectionate, requires warmth.
  • British Shorthair: Round face, independent yet cuddly.
  • Abyssinian: Ticked coat, curious explorer.
  • Scottish Fold: Folded ears, sweet personality.
  • Burmese: Solid color, playful lifelong kitten.

Choose based on lifestyle—active homes suit Bengals, quiet ones Persians. Sources: CFA/TCF breed standards.
